# Economics

## Why This Product Can Work

Many startups and small teams are spending a lot on AI tools without realizing it. This app helps them understand where they are overspending and which AI tools or plans would be more beneficial for their needs.

---

## Estimated Lead Value

Some startups spend thousands of dollars every year on AI subscriptions. If users find useful savings through the audit, they may continue using the platform for future optimization and recommendations.

---

## Customer Acquisition

The product can grow through:

- Product Hunt
- Reddit
- Twitter/X
- founder communities
- social sharing

People may share their audit results if they find useful savings.

---

## Conversion Flow

User visits site → completes audit → sees savings → shares results or returns for future audits.

---

## Growth Opportunity

As AI tool usage increases, more companies will look for ways to reduce AI spending and manage subscriptions better.
