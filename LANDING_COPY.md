## FAQ

### 1. What does CostPilot do?

CostPilot analyzes your AI subscriptions and identifies opportunities to reduce unnecessary spending.

---

### 2. Do I need to create an account?

No. The audit works without signup.

---

### 3. Which AI tools are supported?

The platform currently supports tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, and Windsurf.

---

### 4. How are recommendations generated?

Recommendations are generated using rule-based audit logic combined with pricing analysis and AI-generated summaries.

---

### 5. Is my data stored?

The public audit view excludes personal details such as company names and email addresses.
