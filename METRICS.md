# Metrics

## Main Metric

The main metric for CostPilot would be:

**Number of completed audits**

This is important because users only get value after completing the audit.

---

## Other Important Metrics

### 1. Audit Starts

How many people click on “Start Free Audit”.

---

### 2. Audit Completion

How many users finish the full audit process.

---

### 3. Result Sharing

How many users share their audit results with others.
