# User Interviews

## Interview 1

### Person
Aman — Freelance Developer

### Quotes
- “I honestly don’t track how much I spend on AI tools monthly.”
- “I pay for both ChatGPT and Claude even though I mostly use one.”
- “If a tool clearly showed savings opportunities, I would definitely try it.”

### Most surprising thing
He cared more about convenience than actual pricing.

### What it changed
I focused on making the audit results simple and clear instead of adding too many technical details.

## Interview 2

### Person
Riya — Startup Founder

### Quotes
- “Our team keeps subscribing to AI tools individually.”
- “We probably have overlapping subscriptions.”
- “I would want a shareable report for finance discussions.”

### Most surprising thing
She said teams often lose track of AI spending because subscriptions are spread across employees.

### What it changed
I made the results section feel more UI-friendly and emphasized on total savings clearly.

## Interview 3

### Person
Karan — CS Student + Indie Hacker

### Quotes
- “I use free trials too much and forget which tools I actually need.”
- “Most AI tools feel similar after a point.”
- “A comparison-based recommendation system would be useful.”

### Most surprising thing
He valued workflow familiarity more than feature differences.

### What it changed
I improved the recommendation logic to avoid unnecessary tool switching.
